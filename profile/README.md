# appgraph

Express your application as a graph of specified, controlled behaviors, inputs, outputs, and side effects. An AI of your choice converts the graph into working code, manages changes, and operates the app in production.
Nodes can contain sub-nodes, enabling many layers of abstraction with a node representing a single unit of behavior (like a function), a component of a larger service, or even an entire micro-service. Graphs also specify permissioning, dependencies, and interfaces. 
Appgraphs are expressed in simple JSON, and can be version-controlled like code. Everything is local, and you can interact with appgraph using a CLI. 

Everything is local and non-priopriatary; you really don't need some expensive SaaS subscription to do this stuff. Appgraphs are just code in github or whatever your choice of source control is. Use whatever AI you want.
